ACE Coference Data


10/9/09
ACE is a NIST maintained collection of news documents annotated with named-entity, coreference, and relation information. The data is available through NIST. In our HLT '07 paper we use a non-standard split of the data from 2004. Download hlt2007_split.tgz to see the exact split into training and testing files we used in that paper.
